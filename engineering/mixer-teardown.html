Mixer Product Teardown

CAD SolidWorks Product Design Reverse Engineering

Assignment: Fully understand the inner workings of a consumer product and the thought process that went into designing it. We tore down a mixer, analyzed its use cases and design, and reverse engineered every component in SolidWorks. We built a slideshow presentation and video summary of the project.

Group members: Jevon Coney, Oren Cohen, Andy Navarro
