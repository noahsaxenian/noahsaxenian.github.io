Spectrum Analyzer

January 2024

LabVIEW Signal Processing FFT

Assignment: Build a spectrum analyzer in LabVIEW that offers the ability for the user to generate complex waveforms and compare them, both visually and auditorily, to an inputted waveform.

Finished Product

Front Panel

The major features of the front panel include:

  • The generated signals panel in the top left allows the user to input the frequency, phase, amplitude, and wave type (sine, square, triangle, or sawtooth) of as many waves as they want. It also allows for the application of a highpass, lowpass, bandpass, or bandstop filter.
  • On the top right, the user can open a .wav file or record audio for the inputted duration.
  • Two buttons allow for audio playback of the generated waveform and the user uploaded or recorded waveform.
  • At the bottom, two graphs are displayed. The left shows the raw waveforms while the right shows the amplitudes of each frequency with a Fast Fourier Transform (FFT).
